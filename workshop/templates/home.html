National Conference on Artificial Intelligence

From October 18 to 20, 2020.
University of Tiaret,
Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science,
Department of Computer Science, Tiaret.

The CNIAA is a forum to present new advances and research results in the field of artificial intelligence.
           The conference will bring together different researchers, engineers and scientists in the field of interest across the national territory.
           The conference covers all theoretical and practical aspects of artificial intelligence.
           Authors are invited to contribute to the conference by submitting articles that illustrate research results, projects, surveying and industrial experiences that describe significant advances in the selected themes of the conference.
